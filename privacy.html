Our Ducktastic Commitment to Privacy

At Quackastic Adventures, we take your privacy as seriously as ducks take to water! This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while you enjoy our duck-themed wonderland.

Information We Collect

  • Basic duck preferences (favorite species, preferred pond activities)

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalizing your virtual pond experience
  • Sending duck-related newsletters (only if you've subscribed)
  • Improving our duck-themed features
  • Managing your duck shop purchases

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of duck-related communications
