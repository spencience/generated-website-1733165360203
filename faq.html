Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Quackastic Adventures?

Quackastic Adventures is your premier destination for all things duck-related, featuring a virtual pond, educational content, and a shop full of duck merchandise.

Is membership free?

Yes! Basic membership is completely free. Premium features are available through our Quack+ subscription.

Virtual Pond

How do I feed the virtual ducks?

Click the bread icon in your inventory and drag it to any duck in the pond. Remember, virtual ducks prefer virtual bread!

Can I name my favorite ducks?

Yes! Quack+ members can name up to 5 ducks in the virtual pond.

Shop & Orders

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all physical merchandise. Virtual items are non-refundable.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to most countries. Shipping rates vary by location.
